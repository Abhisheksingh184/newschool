Admissions

Join our community of learners and embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth

Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Fill out the application form either online or collect it from the school office. Submit the completed form along with the required documents and application fee.

2

Entrance Test

Students applying for classes I to XII will have to appear for an entrance test that evaluates their proficiency in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge.

3

Interview

After clearing the entrance test, students and parents will be called for an interview with the Principal and members of the admission committee.

4

Selection

Based on the entrance test results and interview, selected students will be offered admission. The list of selected candidates will be displayed on the school notice board and website.

5

Fee Payment

Selected candidates need to pay the admission fee and submit the required documents within the specified time period to confirm their admission.

Admission Requirements

Age Criteria (as of 31st March of the academic year):

  • Nursery: 3+ years
  • LKG: 4+ years
  • UKG: 5+ years
  • Class I: 6+ years
  • For other classes, corresponding age progression applies

Required Documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs of the student
  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate from the previous school (for Classes II and above)
  • Report card/Progress report of the previous academic year
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar card of the student (photocopy)
  • Residential proof

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. When does the admission process start?

The admission process for the new academic session typically begins in January. However, admissions for certain classes may remain open throughout the year based on seat availability.

Q. Is there a school bus facility available?

Yes, the school provides transportation services covering various routes in and around Jabalpur. The bus fee varies according to the distance from the school.

Q. What is the student-teacher ratio in the school?

We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio of 25:1 to ensure that each student receives adequate attention and guidance.

Q. Does the school offer any scholarships?

Yes, the school offers merit-based scholarships to deserving students who excel in academics, sports, or other extracurricular activities. Additionally, we also have provisions for financial aid for economically disadvantaged students.

Q. What board is the school affiliated with?

Hotumal Kerala High School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi.
