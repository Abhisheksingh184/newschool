Facilities

Discover the state-of-the-art facilities that enhance learning and growth at Hotumal Kerala High School

Our Facilities

Library

Our well-stocked library offers a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to foster a love for reading and research among students.

Science Laboratories

Modern science labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology provide hands-on learning experiences with advanced equipment.

Computer Lab

A fully equipped computer lab with high-speed internet and the latest software to support IT education and digital skills.

Sports Facilities

Spacious playgrounds and courts for football, basketball, cricket, and more, encouraging physical fitness and teamwork.

Auditorium

A large, well-equipped auditorium for school events, performances, and assemblies, fostering creativity and cultural expression.

Transport

Safe and reliable school buses covering various routes in Jabalpur, ensuring convenient commuting for students.
